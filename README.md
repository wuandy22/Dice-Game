🎲 Dice Auction (Working Title)
👥 Players
3–5 players (4 recommended)
🎯 Objective
Win chips by building the most valuable dice combination through auctions and trades.

🧰 Components
3 standard 6-sided dice per player
Chips (each player starts with 100)
🔄 Round Structure
Each round consists of:

Ante
Roll
Auction Phase
Reveal & Payout
1. 💰 Ante
Each player antes 5 chips into the pot
2. 🎲 Roll (Private)
Each player rolls 3 dice privately
Dice remain hidden until revealed or auctioned
3. 🔁 Auction Phase
Starting from a designated first player (rotate each round), players take turns clockwise.

On Your Turn:
Choose one die from your hand
Place it face-up for auction (everyone sees its value)
📣 Open-Outcry Auction Rules
Bidding starts at 0 chips
Players may bid at any time (no strict turn order)
Each new bid must be at least 1 chip higher than the current highest bid
Players may bid multiple times
❌ No-Bid Rule
If no player makes a bid:
The auctioning player takes the die back
No exchange occurs
Play proceeds to the next player
🔄 Exchange (If There Is a Winner)
The winning bidder:

Pays their bid to the auctioning player
Takes the auctioned die
Must give one of their own dice in exchange
🔍 Public Exchange (Full Information)
The auctioned die and the exchanged die are both revealed to all players
🔢 Auction Count
Each player auctions exactly 1 die per round
After all players have auctioned, proceed to scoring
4. 🏆 Reveal & Payout
All players reveal their final 3 dice.

🎯 Scoring
Each of the following earns 1 share of the pot:

🥇 Highest Total
🥈 Lowest Total
🎲 Each 3-of-a-Kind
💰 Pot Distribution
Total shares = number of all awarded shares
The pot is split evenly among shares
📌 Example
Final hands:

Player A: 6-6-6 (sum 18)
Player B: 4-4-4 (sum 12)
Player C: 1-1-1 (sum 3)
Player D: 1-3-4 (sum 8)
Shares:

A: 1 (highest) + 1 (trips) = 2
C: 1 (lowest) + 1 (trips) = 2
B: 1 (trips) = 1
D: 0
Total shares = 5
Each share = pot ÷ 5

⚖️ Ties
Highest / Lowest:
Tied players split the share
Example: 2 players tie → each gets 0.5 shares
3-of-a-Kind:
Each qualifying player gets 1 full share (no splitting)
🔁 Game End
Play for a set number of rounds or until players decide to stop
Player with the most chips wins

🎲 How to Play

Each round, players are dealt hidden dice and take turns auctioning one die to the group. The winning bidder swaps their auctioned die for one from the seller's hand — chips go to the seller.

Scoring (end of each round)

  • Highest total across all dice → 1 share
  • Lowest total across all dice → 1 share
  • Three-of-a-kind → 1 share

Shares are split equally among ties. The pot is divided proportionally by shares earned.

4 Dice Mode

  • 4 dice per player, two auction rounds per player (snake order)
  • Four-of-a-kind → 4 shares (trips don't score)
